# Pi' Conversions
## v1.1.2
### A Python Discord bot to automatically handle conversions between metric and imperial units.
---
#### Accepted Arguments

- `km`
    - Will convert `km` to `mi`
- `mi`
    - Will convert `mi` to `km`
- `kg`
    - Will convert `kg` to `lb`
- `lb`
    - Will convert `lb` to `kg`
- `c`
    - Will convert degrees `c` to degrees `f`
- `f`
    - Will convert degrees `f` to degrees `c`
- `inch` or `inches`
    - Will convert `inches` to `centimeters`
- `cm`
    - Will convert `centimeters` to `inches`
--- 
### Example
```
Message sent in channel or thread:
I ran 10 km yesterday

Message returned:
<user>, 10.0 km is equal to 6.18 mi
```
